
Shop Item Guide: 

In the Item Shop, you can choose one of three items to aid you on your journey. 
Each item has a unique benefit, so choose wisely based on your strategy 
and current needs.

Available Items:

Dagger: Cut out your eye and place it on the scale, 
giving you one point toward victory.

Boulder: Place a Boulder to block enemies' attacks, 
absorbing up to 5 hit points.

Shrimp Bottle: A shrimp card will go right into 
your hand.
